Healthy Child Lifestyle Educational Quiz

Doctor Fit, Action Ace and the entire 5210 League understand the importance of leading a healthy and active lifestyle. Together with everyone’s commitment, your whole family can take the pledge to make healthier choices and become more physically active.
Take this short quiz to better understand your child’s current health routines and then learn ways you can help reinforce their healthy habits.

1. How many servings of fruits and vegetables does your child eat per day? (1/2 cup of raw vegetables or 1 medium sized piece of fruit = 1 serving)
2. How many minutes per day is your child physically active?
3. How often does your child consume sweet drinks (soda, sweet tea, sports drinks, fruit or other juice drinks) in a day?
4. How many eight ounce cups of water does your child drink in a day?
5. How many minutes does your child spend time watching TV, playing video games or using electronics in a day?
